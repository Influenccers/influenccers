Who are we?

Our story

Influenccers was founded with a clear purpose: to connect brands and influencers in a way that delivers real value. What began as an idea to simplify influencer marketing has grown into a trusted network where quality products meet authentic voices.
We focus on building partnerships that work. From researching audience fit to tracking campaign results, our approach is designed to create opportunities for growth.

We as a team

  • Bring together expertise in product selection, campaign strategy, and affiliate collaboration.

  • We’re researchers who identify products with potential, strategists who craft effective campaigns, and managers who ensure every partnership runs smoothly.

  • With a shared commitment to clarity and results, we handle the details so brands and influencers can focus on what they do best.
